# 2024 – St. Louis: Loop Mural Festival

**Year:** 2024 (May 31)  
**Location:** Delmar Loop, St. Louis, Missouri, US  
**Artist:** Ron English  

![Ron English – Loop Mural Festival 2024, St. Louis](../images/Murals/Loop-Festival.jpg)

## Description

Ron English participated in the **2024 Loop Mural Festival** in St. Louis, contributing a mural unveiled around **May 31, 2024**. His involvement is confirmed by local news coverage and a direct Instagram post announcing “Hello St Louis!” with festival hashtags. Contemporary reporting highlights the festival’s efforts to revitalize the Delmar Loop district through large-scale public artworks.
