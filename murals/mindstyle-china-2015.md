# 2015 – MC Supersized & Grin mural (MINDstyle Headquarters, China)

**Year:** 2015  
**Location:** MINDstyle / Pop Life Global Headquarters, China  

![Ron English – MC Supersized mural at MINDstyle Headquarters, China (2015)](../images/Murals/Mindstyle-China-2015.jpg)

This mural features Ron English’s iconic **MC Supersized** and **Grin** characters, painted inside the **MINDstyle / Pop Life Global** headquarters in China. English partnered extensively with MINDstyle during this period, creating figures, packaging art and large-scale mural installations throughout their Asian facilities. The Nov 23, 2015 post documenting this wall is part of that long-running collaboration.

![MINDstyle – view 1](../images/Murals/Mindstyle-China-2015.jpg)
