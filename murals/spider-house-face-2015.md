# 2015 – “Grin” façade mural at Spider House (SXSW/RXSM)

**Year:** 2015  
**Location:** Spider House, Austin, Texas, US  

## Description

During the SXSW/RXSM festivities in mid-March 2015, Ron English painted this standalone **“Grin”** mural on one of Spider House’s exterior façades. Distinct from the large collaborative wall he completed with Risk, this piece centers on English’s trademark Smiley-skull hybrid rendered against a vivid gradient background. It was painted simultaneously with several other works installed across the Spider House property during the festival.
