# 2018 – Wynwood Walls (Basel Week)

**Year:** 2018  
**Location:** Wynwood Walls, Miami, Florida  
**Event:** Miami Art Week / Art Basel programming

During Miami Art Week 2018, Wynwood Walls expanded its annual Basel-season programming with live painting sessions and a roster mixing established muralists with rising international artists.  
English contributed new POPaganda imagery to the complex, with press coverage noting how his work helped anchor that year's refreshed visual momentum across Wynwood.
