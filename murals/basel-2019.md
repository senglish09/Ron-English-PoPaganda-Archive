## Details
**Year:** 2019  
**Location:** Wynwood & Miami Design District, Miami, Florida, US  
**Event:** Art Basel Miami Week / Miami Art Week  
**Title:** *Delusionville mural (Art Basel 2019)*  

## Comment
Large black-and-white Delusionville composition painted across multiple days during Art Basel Miami Week 2019. Ron shared daily progress shots (Nov 28–Dec 1), showing the wall in Wynwood / Design District gradually filling with classic characters including the skunk, unicorn, ram, vulture, and blue pig. The mural became a popular stop for Basel visitors and photographers.
