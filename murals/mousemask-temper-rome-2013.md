# 2013 – Temper Tot & Mousemask Murphy  
**Quadraro, Rome (M.U.Ro project)**

**Year:** 2013  
**Location:** Via dei Pisoni 89, Quadraro, Rome, Italy  

Painted for Rome’s open-air museum **M.U.Ro (Museo di Urban Art di Roma)**, this large mural pairs two of Ron English’s signature characters: the hyper-muscled **Temper Tot** and the gas-masked **Mousemask Murphy**. Italian press interpreted the duo as an allegory of a powerful nation guided by immature leadership, while local guides emphasized how the flat pastel backdrop pushes both figures forward in near-sculptural relief. The piece later appeared in Sky Arte’s documentary *Muro a Roma* and is now one of Quadraro’s most visited street-art landmarks.
