# *Translations* — Allouche Gallery (Los Angeles, 2024)

### **Solo Exhibition**

<img src="../../../images/Exhibitions/Solo/Translations, 2024.jpg"
     alt="Ron English — Translations, Allouche Gallery, Los Angeles, 2024"
     class="exhibit-image">

**Year:** 2024  
**Dates:** June 13 – July 9, 2024  
**Venue:** Allouche Gallery  
**Location:** Los Angeles, California  
**Title:** *Translations*

---

## Overview

Solo exhibition at Allouche Gallery in Los Angeles presenting new paintings and sculptural works by Ron English. *Translations* continues English’s POPaganda language by evolving characters, motifs and visual codes into newly hybrid compositions.
