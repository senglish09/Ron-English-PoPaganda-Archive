## Overview

Solo exhibition at **Allouche Gallery** presenting recent paintings, installations and POPaganda imagery expanding English’s investigations into perception and illusion. The show highlighted **optical shifts, layered characters, and unseen cultural forces**, exploring the idea of “now you see it” across both painting and sculptural forms during New York’s spring art season.
