# *Sugar Circus*  
### The Nest, Q Plex, Shenzhen (2021–2022)

### **Solo Exhibition**

**Years:** 2021–2022  
**Dates:** November 19, 2021 – January 1, 2022  
**Venue:** The Nest, Q Plex  
**Location:** Shenzhen, China  
**Title:** *Sugar Circus*

---

## Overview

Immersive solo exhibition at **The Nest / Q Plex** in Shenzhen, featuring **candy-culture iconography**, large-scale installations, and hyper-color environments.  
English builds on his recurring themes of **consumption, desire, youth marketing, and pop-surrealism**, transforming the venue into a sugar-saturated circus world connected to the broader POPaganda universe.
