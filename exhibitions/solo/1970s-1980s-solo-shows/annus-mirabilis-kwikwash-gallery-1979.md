# c. 1979 – *Annus ... Mirabilis* (Kwikwash Gallery, Houston)

**Year:** c. 1979 (December 7)  
**Venue:** Kwikwash Gallery  
**Location:** Houston, Texas, US  
**Title:** *Annus ... Mirabilis*  

![Ron English – “Eye/Hand Coordination — Annus ... Mirabilis”, Kwikwash Gallery invitation](../../images/Exhibitions/Solo/quickwas.jpg)

## Notes

Photography-based exhibition titled **“Eye/Hand Coordination — Annus ... Mirabilis”** at Kwikwash Gallery, 3207 Montrose, Houston. The invitation announces an evening opening on **7 December**, from **7–10 p.m.**, highlighting English’s early experiments with photographic work and the coordination between perception (“eye”) and manual image-making (“hand”).

The show marks one of his first documented solo presentations in Texas, setting up themes he later expanded through staged photographic tableaux and POPaganda imagery.

## Sources

- Invitation card: *Eye/Hand Coordination — Annus ... Mirabilis* (Kwikwash Gallery, 3207 Montrose, Houston, opening 7 Dec 1979, 7–10 p.m.).
