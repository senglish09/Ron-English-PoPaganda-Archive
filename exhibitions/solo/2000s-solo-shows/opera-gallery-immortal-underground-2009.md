## Overview

*Immortal Underground* opened at **Opera Gallery’s SoHo location** on **November 12, 2009**, presenting a theatrical installation of Ron English’s newest Popaganda works. The show featured large-format portraits, vivid pop-culture hybrids and stage-like gallery design elements that emphasized English’s ongoing explorations of fame, mythology and media distortion.

Press coverage highlighted the dramatic presentation and strong attendance at the opening. The exhibition reinforced English’s role as a central figure in the New York Pop Surrealism and contemporary Pop Art scenes during the late 2000s, following his earlier 2008 *Abraham Obama* presentation at the same gallery.
