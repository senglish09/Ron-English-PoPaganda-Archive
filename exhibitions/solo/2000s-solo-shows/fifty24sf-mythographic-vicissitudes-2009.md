---

## Overview

*Mythographic Vicissitudes* opened on **January 8, 2009** at San Francisco’s FIFTY24SF Gallery, presenting a focused body of new figurative works by Ron English. The exhibition expanded upon English’s ongoing exploration of mythic symbolism, pop-culture distortion and hyperreal consumer archetypes.

Preview coverage highlighted the show’s hybrid allegorical approach — blending reimagined cartoon figures, Americana, religious tropes and saturated, pseudo-mythological visuals. Opening-night reports also noted its pairing at the gallery alongside Alex Pardee’s concurrent show, creating a high-attendance early-2009 program at Upper Playground’s flagship art space.
