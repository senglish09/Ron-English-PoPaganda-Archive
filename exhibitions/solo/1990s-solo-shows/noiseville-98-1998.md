# *Noiseville 98* — Noiseville Gallery, New York (1998)

### **Solo Exhibition**

<img src="../../images/Exhibitions/Solo/noiseville.jpg"
     alt="Ron English — Noiseville 98, Noiseville Gallery, 1998"
     class="exhibit-image">

**Year:** 1998  
**Dates:** July 31 – August 16, 1998  
**Venue:** Noiseville Gallery  
**Location:** New York, New York, USA  
**Title:** *Noiseville 98*

---

## Overview

*Noiseville 98* showcased a striking selection of Ron English’s late-1990s work, including one of the earliest documented appearances of his **crucified Mickey Mouse on a mousetrap**—a now-iconic Popaganda image critiquing Disney’s cultural dominance and the commercialization of innocence.

Presented at **Noiseville Gallery**, a downtown space known for its ties to underground culture and alternative music, the exhibition highlighted English’s ability to merge visual satire with punk sensibilities. The show also aligned with the gallery’s ethos of pushing confrontational, countercultural themes into the art world.
