# *Art in Heaven* — Michael Kisslinger Gallery (1994)

### **Solo Exhibition**

<img src="../../images/Exhibitions/Solo/kisslinger.jpg"
     alt="Ron English — Art in Heaven, Michael Kisslinger Gallery, 1994"
     class="exhibit-image">

**Year:** 1994  
**Dates:** June 30 – July 30, 1994  
**Venue:** Michael Kisslinger Gallery  
**Location:** New York, New York, USA  
**Title:** *Art in Heaven*

---

## Overview

This installment of **Art in Heaven** brought Ron English’s traveling solo exhibition to the **Michael Kisslinger Gallery** in SoHo. Opening on **June 30, 1994**, the presentation continued the touring format launched earlier that year in Austin.

The exhibition featured paintings, drawings and photographs reflecting English’s interest in the intersection of belief systems, media conditioning and visual satire. The New York venue provided a broader platform at a moment when English’s Popaganda style was gaining reach within the downtown art scene.
