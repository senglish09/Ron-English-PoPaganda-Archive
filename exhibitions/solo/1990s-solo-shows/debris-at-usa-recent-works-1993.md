# *Recent Works by Ron English* — Debris at USA / Big Bottom Lounge (1993)

### **Solo Exhibition**

<img src="../../images/Exhibitions/Solo/debris.jpg"
     alt="Ron English — Recent Works, Debris at USA (Big Bottom Lounge), 1993"
     class="exhibit-image">

**Year:** 1993  
**Date:** March 24, 1993  
**Venue:** Debris at USA (Big Bottom Lounge)  
**Location:** New York, New York, USA  
**Title:** *Recent Works by Ron English*

---

## Overview

Staged inside the **Big Bottom Lounge** at **Debris at Club USA** in Times Square, this one-night presentation gathered a tight selection of Ron English’s early-1990s paintings under the banner *Recent Works by Ron English*.

The event format blurred the line between nightclub and gallery: English’s canvases—already steeped in billboard aesthetics, cartoon distortion and pop-culture critique—were shown in a nightlife setting rather than a traditional white cube. The show card positions the evening as a focused snapshot of his practice at the time, capturing the moment when his Popaganda language was gaining momentum in New York’s downtown and club scenes.
