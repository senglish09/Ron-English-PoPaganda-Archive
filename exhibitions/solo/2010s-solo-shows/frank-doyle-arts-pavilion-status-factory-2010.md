**Year:** 2010  
**Dates:** November 12 – December 17, 2010  
**Venue:** Frank M. Doyle Arts Pavilion (Orange Coast College)  
**Location:** Costa Mesa, California, US  
**Title:** *Status Factory – The Art of Ron English*

---

## Overview

Large museum expansion of Ron English’s *Status Factory* project featuring **over 100 works**, expanding the exhibition format seen in earlier presentations into a comprehensive survey of the artist’s pop-surrealist and POPaganda practice during the late 2000s and early 2010s.

The exhibition took place at the **Frank M. Doyle Arts Pavilion** at Orange Coast College in Costa Mesa, offering an academic museum context for English’s work and marking a significant institutional presentation of his artistic trajectory.
