### Tribeca Grand Hotel (Natalie Kates Projects), New York, NY (2014)

**Year:** 2014

**Venue:** Tribeca Grand Hotel (Natalie Kates Projects)  
**Location:** New York, NY  
**Title:** *Evolutionary Alternatives*

---

## Overview

Solo presentation organized by **Natalie Kates Projects** at the Tribeca Grand Hotel, highlighting English’s evolving POPaganda mythologies and character worlds, with a particular emphasis on hybrid evolution themes across creatures and consumer icons.
