# *Ron English X Secret Fresh*  
### Secret Fresh Gallery (Ronac Art Center), Manila, Philippines (2014)

### **Solo Exhibition**
     
<img src="../../../images/Exhibitions/Solo/secret fresh dec 2014 8.jpg"
     alt="Ron English X Secret Fresh, Manila, 2014"
     class="exhibit-image">

**Year:** 2014  
**Date:** December 21, 2014  
**Venue:** Secret Fresh Gallery (Ronac Art Center)  
**Location:** Manila, Philippines  
**Title:** *Ron English X Secret Fresh*

---

## Overview

Holiday-season exhibition featuring major **Delusionville-related** works, releases and media coverage—continuing English’s long-standing collaboration with **Secret Fresh** and connecting POPaganda themes with Manila’s designer-toy culture.
