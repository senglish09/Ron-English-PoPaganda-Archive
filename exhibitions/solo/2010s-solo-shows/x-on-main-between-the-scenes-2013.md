**Location:** Beacon, NY  
**Title:** *Between the Scenes – X On Main*

---

## Overview

At **X on Main**, English unveiled **his first new photographic series in 25 years**. These photographs are used as **studies for his paintings**, themselves based on complex dioramas he constructs in his studio. The exhibition foregrounds the relationship between staged photography, process, and POPaganda painting.
