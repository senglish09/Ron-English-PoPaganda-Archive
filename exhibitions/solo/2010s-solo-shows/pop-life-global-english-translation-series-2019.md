# *English Translation Series*  
### Pop Life Global Q-Plex, Shenzhen (2019)

### **Solo Exhibition**

<img src="../../../images/Exhibitions/Solo/English Translation Series, 2019.jpg"
     alt="Ron English — English Translation Series, Pop Life Global Q-Plex, Shenzhen, 2019"
     class="exhibit-image">

**Year:** 2019  
**Dates:** June 21 – July 14, 2019  
**Venue:** Pop Life Global Q-Plex  
**Location:** Shenzhen, China  
**Title:** *English Translation Series*

---

## Overview

Solo exhibition presenting Ron English’s **“English Translation Series”** including canvases, sculptures, and designer-toy works installed in an immersive environment at **Pop Life Global’s Q-Plex** venue in Shenzhen.
