# *Universal Grin*  
### Galerie Matthew Namour, Montreal, Canada (2018)

### **Solo Exhibition**

<img src="../../../images/Exhibitions/Solo/universal-grin.jpg"
     alt="Ron English — Universal Grin, Galerie Matthew Namour, Montreal, 2018"
     class="exhibit-image">

**Year:** 2018  
**Dates:** October 18 – November 18, 2018  
**Venue:** Galerie Matthew Namour  
**Location:** Montreal, Canada  
**Title:** *Universal Grin*

---

## Overview

Solo exhibition in Old Montreal focusing on Ron English’s **“Grin”** iconography, expanding the motif across paintings, editions and sculptural objects, staged in Galerie Matthew Namour’s contemporary art program.
