**Location:** Tokyo, Japan  
**Title:** *POPaganda in Japan*

---

## Overview

Ron English’s **first Japan solo project**, presented with **ZacPac**, unveiling new paintings alongside collaboration releases including a limited **Be@rbrick**, marking English’s debut in Tokyo’s designer-toy and pop-art crossover scene.
