**Location:** New York City, US  
**Title:** *Delusionville*

---

## Overview

Exhibition presenting Ron English’s **latest studio paintings and sculptural works**, centered on Delusionville’s expanding mythos. A collaborative sub-section, **“Rival Verses,”** created with **Daniel Johnston**, highlights decades of shared creative exchange and mutual inspiration.
