**Title:** *Status Factory By Ron English*

---

## Overview

Immersive pop-up “factory” installation in a **SoHo warehouse during Fashion Week**, transforming the exhibition space into a fully branded POPaganda environment including life-size figures, interactive objects, custom lighting and billboard-scale scenography.
