### South Texas College — Pecan Campus Library Art Gallery (2011)

**Year:** 2011  
**Dates:** March 28 – April 15, 2011  
**Venue:** South Texas College, Pecan Campus Library Art Gallery  
**Location:** McAllen, Texas, US  
**Title:** *The Other Side of Madison Avenue*  

---

## Overview

Solo exhibition at **South Texas College’s Pecan Campus Library Art Gallery**, presenting works that critique advertising and mass-media aesthetics through English’s “popaganda” style. Framed as an educational showcase, the exhibition offered students and visitors an introduction to English’s satirical treatment of advertising culture and corporate iconography.
