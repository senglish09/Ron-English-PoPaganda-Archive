# *Garden of Mutant Plants*  
### JPS Gallery (Repulse Bay｜The Pulse), Hong Kong (2017)

### **Solo Exhibition**

<img src="../../../images/Exhibitions/Solo/mutantplants.jpg"
     alt="Ron English — Garden of Mutant Plants, JPS Gallery, Hong Kong, 2017"
     class="exhibit-image">

**Year:** 2017  
**Dates:** March 19 – April 19, 2017  
**Venue:** JPS Gallery (Repulse Bay｜The Pulse)  
**Location:** Hong Kong, China  
**Title:** *Garden of Mutant Plants*

---

## Overview

Immersive installation transforming the gallery into a fantastical **mutant-plant garden**, expanding English’s POPaganda mythos into living sculptural forms, botanical hybrids, and theatrical environments within Repulse Bay’s contemporary art setting.
