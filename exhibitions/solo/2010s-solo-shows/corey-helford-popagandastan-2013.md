**Year:** 2013  
**Dates:** October 26 – November 16, 2013  
**Venue:** Corey Helford Gallery  
**Location:** Los Angeles, California, US  
**Title:** *POPAGANDASTAN – New Works from Ron English*

---

## Overview

Large solo exhibition expanding English’s **satirical universe of brand icons and mythic figures**, pushing the POPaganda aesthetic through monumental canvases, characters, and commercial parody in a major Los Angeles setting.
