# *Do estúdio para a rua (From Studio to Street)*  
### CAIXA Cultural (Galleries 2–3), Rio de Janeiro, Brazil (2014)

### **Solo Exhibition**

<img src="../../../images/Exhibitions/Solo/a-south-american-butterfly-SM-Copy-scaled.jpg"
     alt="Ron English — Do estúdio para a rua, CAIXA Cultural, Rio de Janeiro, 2014"
     class="exhibit-image">

**Year:** 2014  
**Dates:** October 21 – December 21, 2014  
**Venue:** CAIXA Cultural (Galleries 2–3)  
**Location:** Rio de Janeiro, Brazil  
**Title:** *Do estúdio para a rua (From Studio to Street)*

---

## Overview

Major Brazilian solo presentation surveying English’s **studio painting**, **public works**, and **POPaganda figuration**, connecting his billboard subversions to Brazilian street-visual traditions, launched in CAIXA Cultural’s prominent Rio de Janeiro gallery spaces.
