# *English 101*  
### Post No Bills, Venice, California (2011)

### **Solo Exhibition**
     
<img src="../../../images/Exhibitions/Solo/English 101, 2011.jpg"
     alt="Ron English — English 101 at Post No Bills, Venice Beach, 2011"
     class="exhibit-image">

**Year:** 2011  
**Dates:** October 6 – 30, 2011  
**Venue:** Post No Bills  
**Location:** Venice, California, US  
**Title:** *English 101*

---

## Overview

Editioned prints, toys, sculptures, and billboard-scale graphics presented at **Post No Bills** in Venice Beach — extending English’s POPaganda aesthetic into the surf-and-skate cultural context with a strong focus on editioned work and graphic installations.
