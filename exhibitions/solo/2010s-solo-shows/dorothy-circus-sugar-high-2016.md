# *Sugar High*
### Dorothy Circus Gallery, Rome, Italy (2016)

### **Solo Exhibition**

**Year:** 2016  
**Dates:** February 20 – March 31, 2016  
**Venue:** Dorothy Circus Gallery  
**Location:** Rome, Italy  
**Title:** *Sugar High*

---

## Overview

Focusing on **overconsumption**, marketing imagery and candy-coated mascots, *Sugar High* presented hybrid figures and sculptural interventions critiquing global eating habits and childhood-targeted advertising, staged in the context of Rome’s Dorothy Circus Gallery.
