**Title:** *Mojo Voodoo / Heavy Metal Muzick*

---

## Overview

Solo exhibition at **Red Truck Gallery** presenting Ron English’s mix of **voodoo iconography, music themes and pop-surrealist characters**, incorporating hybrid figures, mystical motifs, and references to New Orleans cultural history. Works blend psychedelia, pop culture, and parody within English’s broader POPaganda universe.
