# *Ron English / Guernica*  
### Allouche Gallery, New York, NY (2016)

### **Solo Exhibition**

<img src="../../../images/Exhibitions/Solo/Ron English - Guernica, 2016  (6).jpg"
     alt="Ron English — Guernica, Allouche Gallery, New York, 2016"
     class="exhibit-image">

**Year:** 2016  
**Dates:** September 22 – October 19, 2016  
**Venue:** Allouche Gallery  
**Location:** New York, New York, US  
**Title:** *Ron English / Guernica*

---

## Overview

Solo exhibition in Allouche Gallery’s Meatpacking District space featuring eighteen new paintings reworking **Picasso’s “Guernica”** as commentary on contemporary political cycles, media spectacle, violence, and reconstruction in the age of global imagery.
