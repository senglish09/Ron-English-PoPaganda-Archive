### Corey Helford Gallery, Culver City (Los Angeles), California (2011)

**Year:** 2011  
**Dates:** November 19 – December 10, 2011  
**Venue:** Corey Helford Gallery  
**Location:** Culver City (Los Angeles), California, US  
**Title:** *Seasons in Supurbia*

---

## Overview

Eighteen-painting series critiquing suburban consumer culture through English’s **POPaganda lens**, approaching the American suburban landscape as an archetype of commercial saturation and visual indoctrination.
