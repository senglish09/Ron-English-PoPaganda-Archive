---

## Overview

Solo museum exhibition at the **International Museum of Art & Science (IMAS)** exploring themes of **illusion, presence and mass-media narratives** within English’s POPaganda universe. The show foregrounded paradox and perception, offering a museum-scale platform for his layered critique of commercial imagery.
