### Lazarides (Rathbone Place), London, UK (2011)

**Year:** 2011  
**Dates:** June 24 – July 21, 2011  
**Venue:** Lazarides (Rathbone Place)  
**Location:** London, UK  
**Title:** *Skin Deep: Post-Instinctual Afterthoughts on Psychological Portraiture*

---

## Overview

Exhibition of **skull-portrait hybrids** exploring psychological portraiture and the conceptual layer between physical appearance and subconscious identity, reflecting English’s ongoing hybridization of cultural portrait forms.
