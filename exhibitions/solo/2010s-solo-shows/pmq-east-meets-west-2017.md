# *East Meets West*  
### The Qube, PMQ, Hong Kong (2017)

### **Solo Exhibition**

**Year:** 2017  
**Dates:** May 6–14, 2017  
**Venue:** The Qube, PMQ  
**Location:** Hong Kong, China  
**Title:** *East Meets West*

---

## Overview

Hong Kong solo exhibition at **The Qube, PMQ**, presenting POPaganda billboards, paintings, and exclusive collaboration merchandise under the theme of cultural crossover. The one-week Central venue activation included limited editions, apparel and project tie-ins linking East–West cultural imagery.
