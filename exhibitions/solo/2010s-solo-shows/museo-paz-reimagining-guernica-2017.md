**Year:** 2017  
**Dates:** April 4 – December 10, 2017  
**Venue:** Museo de la Paz de Gernika  
**Location:** Gernika-Lumo, Bizkaia, Spain  
**Title:** *Ron English: Reimagining Guernica*

---

## Overview

Solo museum project marking the **80th anniversary of the bombing of Gernika**, presenting Ron English’s reinterpretations of Picasso’s *Guernica*. Through mural-scale works and expanded visual narratives, the exhibition situates English’s POPaganda lens within themes of memory, trauma, and contemporary political conflict.
