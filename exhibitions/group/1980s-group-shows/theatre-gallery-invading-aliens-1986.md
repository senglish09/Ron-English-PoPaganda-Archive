# *Invading Aliens from the Beach*  
### Theatre Gallery, Dallas (1986)

### **Group / Collaborative Exhibition**

<img src="../../images/Exhibitions/Group/invading-aliens copy.jpg"
     alt="Invading Aliens from the Beach at Theatre Gallery, Dallas, 1986"
     class="exhibit-image">

**Year:** 1986  
**Date:** April 5, 1986  
**Venue:** Theatre Gallery  
**Location:** Dallas, Texas, US  
**Title:** *Invading Aliens from the Beach*  

---

## Overview  

One-night collaborative presentation by **Susan English** and **Ron English**, featuring their early “alien” and hybrid-figure imagery. This short-run event reflects the couple’s evolving collaboration in Dallas during the mid-1980s, combining photography, staged compositions and science-fiction themes.

Documentation survives primarily through the original **showcard** ephemera.
