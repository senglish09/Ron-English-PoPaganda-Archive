**Title:** *Freedom of Expression Show*

---

## Overview

Organized by Ron English and Shawn Eichman with the Emergency Committee on the Flag Burning Case, *The Freedom of Expression Show* featured artists responding to national imagery and constitutional debates. The showcard highlights participation by a wide roster of downtown figures including English, Martin Wong, Chris Huestis, and others. Framed around first-amendment concerns and cultural protest, the event used club space as an activist art venue.
