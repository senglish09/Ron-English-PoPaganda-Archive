# *Dubious Edge*  
### Theatre Gallery, Dallas (1985)

### **Group Exhibition**

     alt="Dubious Edge at Theatre Gallery, Dallas, 1985"
     class="exhibit-image">

**Year:** 1985  
**Dates:** August 3–30, 1985  
**Venue:** Theatre Gallery  
**Location:** Dallas, Texas, US  
**Title:** *Dubious Edge*  

---

## Overview  

**Dubious Edge** featured four Texas-based artists working across photography, painting, and mixed-media. Ron English participated as both photographer and video collaborator, debuting **Drip Aesthetic**, a work that explored vandal-billboard culture and apocalyptic language.

The opening included performance and live music, reflecting Dallas’ interconnected underground art subcultures in the mid-1980s.
