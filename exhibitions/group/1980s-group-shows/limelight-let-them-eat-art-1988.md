**Date:** October 26, 1988  
**Venue:** Limelight  
**Location:** New York, NY  
**Title:** *Let Them Eat Art!*

---

## Overview
One-night club-culture extravaganza presenting “Armamentariumism,” an English-framed neo-movement bringing together downtown artists, performance traditions and media parody. The event combined showcard graphics, performance and an invitation announcing English’s publications and video works, including a new collaboration with Shawn Eichman.

Artists and performers listed included Susan English, Shawn Eichman, Ena Kostabi, Brandi Merolla, and Randall Hart (Ron English’s pseudonym), among others.
