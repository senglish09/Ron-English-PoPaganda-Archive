**Year:** 1986  
**Dates:** October 1986  
**Venue:** Houston Center for Photography  
**Location:** Houston, Texas, US  
**Title:** *Texas 150: New Texas Photography*  

---

## Overview

Group exhibition of twelve Houston photographers showcasing new directions in documentary and conceptual photographic practice during the mid-1980s.  

Ron English’s work was noted for its examination of **Texas political and social landscapes**, with a focus on urban signage, roadside billboards, and public visual media. His photographs reframed everyday advertising in critical ways, revealing how mass-media language shaped local identity.
