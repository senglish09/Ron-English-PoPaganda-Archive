## Overview  

Organized by Ron English, **Staff Infection** presented work by Mark Kostabi’s studio assistants, informally referred to as *“The Ghosts of Kostabi.”* The exhibition highlighted behind-the-scenes contributors to Kostabi’s production practice, reframing the idea of authorship and authorship labor in New York’s 1980s art scene.
