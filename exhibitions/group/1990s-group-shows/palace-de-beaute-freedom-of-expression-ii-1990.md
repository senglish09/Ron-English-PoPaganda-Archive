# *Freedom of Expression II*  
### Palace de Beauté, New York (1990)

<img src="../images/Exhibitions/Group/freedom-of-exp2.jpg"
     alt="Freedom of Expression II at Palace de Beauté, New York, 1990"
     class="exhibit-image">

**Year:** 1990  
**Date:** July 6, 1990  
**Venue:** Palace de Beauté  
**Location:** New York City, US  

---

## Overview

Invited group exhibition presented by **English & Eichman**, focusing on “flag art” and political expression, staged as a cultural protest against proposed constitutional amendments restricting flag-related expression. The show included approximately **30 American artists**, with a satirical “revolutionary” presentation and political-fashion components.
