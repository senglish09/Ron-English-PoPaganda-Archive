# 2023 – *Delusionville Mix–N–Match Series 2* (Shping × Ron English)

**Year:** 2023 (February 10, 2023)  
**Platform:** Shping  
**Type:** Character-based NFT series  

![Delusionville Mix–N–Match Series 2](../images/Digital/mixnmatch2.webp)

## Overview

Following the success of Series 1, **Mix–N–Match Series 2** introduced new Delusionville characters and expanded the world’s internal logic. This set built on the collectible “puzzle” format—each NFT is a standalone character, but assembling multiple pieces reveals deeper symbolic themes tied to Delusionville’s social hierarchy and twisted folklore.

Series 2 placed more emphasis on **role reversals, hybrid species and exaggerated moral archetypes**, reflecting Ron English’s approach to reframing fables through a Popaganda lens. Several characters introduced here later reappeared in VeVe’s 2024–25 audio drops and in Shping’s 2023–24 utility expansions.

Collectors praised the series for its bold colors, high-resolution animation loops and the way it fleshed out previously minor characters from English’s universe.
