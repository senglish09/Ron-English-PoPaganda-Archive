# 2025 – Ron English — *Fauxlosophy #1* (VeVe Digital Comic)

**Year:** 2025 (drop date May 14, 2025)  
**Platform:** VeVe – Digital Comics  
**Type:** NFT comic book debut (multiple cover rarities; blind-box release)  

![Ron English — Fauxlosophy #1 (VeVe comic cover)](../images/Digital/fauxlosophy1.jpg)

## Overview

**“Fauxlosophy #1”** marks the debut of Ron English’s long-running *Fauxlosophy* series in comic-book form. Known for its sharp, quotable one-liners and Popaganda wit, the project shifts from static prints and gallery pieces into a collectible digital comic structured around VeVe’s blind-box format. Readers receive one of several rarity covers, each offering a different visual interpretation of English’s satirical “quotable quotes.”

The release positions *Fauxlosophy* as a standalone narrative and collectible line within VeVe, separate from English’s Delusionville or Cereal Killers worlds. By adapting the punchline-driven format into a comic layout, the drop gives *Fauxlosophy* a new life as an episodic digital publication—with the potential for future issues to expand the catalog of English’s signature aphorisms.
