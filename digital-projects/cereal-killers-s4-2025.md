# 2025 – *Ron English — Cereal Killers Series 4* (VeVe)

**Year:** 2025 (June 8, 2025)  
**Platform:** VeVe – Cereal Killers  
**Type:** Blind-box 3D collectible series  

![Ron English — Cereal Killers S4](../images/Digital/cerealkillers4.webp)

## Overview

**Cereal Killers Series 4** was marketed as the **“Last Supper” set**, rounding out the breakfast-mascot saga that had been unfolding across multiple VeVe drops since 2021. This wave introduced two major new characters—**Poo Fairy** and **Coco Paunch**—adding both humor and chaos to the increasingly dense Popaganda breakfast universe.

S4 completes the long-running table of mascots referenced throughout the line, tying directly into earlier pieces like *Last Fat Breakfast* and culminating in the later *Last Supper Diorama*. Collectors view this series as both the thematic and narrative capstone of the Cereal Killers digital line.
