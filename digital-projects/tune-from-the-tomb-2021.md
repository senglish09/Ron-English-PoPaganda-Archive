# 2021 – *A Tune from the Tomb / Jeremiah Grin*  
**Superchief Gallery NFT × Daniel Johnston Estate × Ron English × Clutter Studios**

**Year:** 2021 (August 27, 2021)  
**Platform:** Superchief Gallery NFT (New York)  
**Type:** Collaborative NFT drop + physical gallery event  
**Image file:** `tune-from-the-tomb.webp`

![A Tune from the Tomb — Jeremiah Grin](../images/Digital/tune-from-the-tomb.webp)

## Overview

*“A Tune from the Tomb”* marks a significant crossover between Ron English, the Daniel Johnston estate and Clutter Studios. The project centers on **Jeremiah Grin**, a character born from Johnston’s iconography and reimagined through English’s Popaganda style.

The Superchief Gallery event functioned as both a **physical exhibition** and a **live NFT launch**, accompanied by a parallel MakersPlace auction. English’s reinterpretation adds sculptural volume, brighter color logic and the signature Grin aesthetic, bridging Johnston’s outsider-art legacy with Popaganda’s visual language.

This drop is remembered for its emotional weight — honoring Johnston’s memory — and for its role in establishing Superchief Gallery’s identity as the first dedicated physical NFT gallery space.
