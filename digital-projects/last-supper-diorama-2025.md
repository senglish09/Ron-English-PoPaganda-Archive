# 2025 – *Ron English — Last Supper Diorama* (VeVe)

**Year:** 2025 (July 13, 2025)  
**Platform:** VeVe – Cereal Killers: The Last Supper  
**Type:** 3D diorama environment  

![Ron English — Last Supper Diorama](../images/Digital/lastsupper.webp)

## Overview

The **Last Supper Diorama** serves as the grand display environment designed specifically for the full Cereal Killers figure set. Inspired by the *Last Fat Breakfast* painting and the S4 “Last Supper” drop, the diorama reimagines the iconic table setting as a **chaotic Popaganda banquet** inside VeVe’s AR-ready ecosystem.

The environment features detailed seating arrangements, stylized table elements, and a series of environmental cues referencing past drops, creating a definitive home for Fat Tony, Poo Fairy, Count Calorie, Coco Paunch and the rest of the breakfast mascots.  
Collectors consider this one of the most polished and conceptually complete digital environments associated with Ron English on VeVe.
