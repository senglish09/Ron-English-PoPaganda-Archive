# 2022 – *The Grand Delusion — Part 1* (Nifty Gateway)

**Year:** 2022 (May 9, 2022)  
**Platform:** Nifty Gateway – Curated Drop  
**Type:** Multi-artwork NFT series (with airdrops)

![Ron English — The Grand Delusion: Part 1](../images/Digital/granddelusion1.webp)

## Overview

**The Grand Delusion — Part 1** expands the Delusionville mythos into a multi-piece NFT collection centered around English’s surreal characters and symbolic worldbuilding. Anchored by the **“Brain Heart”** artwork and accompanied by a range of character-driven pieces, the drop explores themes of emotional distortion, propaganda logic, and fractured identity within English’s Popaganda universe.

The release also included **airdrop rewards** tied to collected pieces, which helped cement it as a highly interactive and community-driven drop. Many collectors consider this the strongest early Delusionville digital release outside of VeVe’s platform.

The series helped set the stage for future expansions of Delusionville across both digital and physical formats, marking a significant point in English’s crossover into crypto-native storytelling.
