# 2022 – Ron English — *Ukraine Relief Project* (VeVe)

**Year:** 2022 (March 12–13, 2022)  
**Platform:** VeVe – Special Charity Drop  
**Type:** Charity NFT release benefiting Ukraine refugee relief  

![Ron English — Ukraine Relief Project](../images/Digital/ukrainerelief.webp)

## Overview

VeVe’s **Ukraine Relief Project** featured two special Ron English releases:  
- **Grade School Guernica 2022**  
- **Sunflower Grin — Special Mission (two variants)**  

The drop was created in response to the 2022 invasion of Ukraine, with **net proceeds donated to the New Zealand Red Cross**. English reinterpreted themes of war and innocence through his Popaganda lens, connecting the historical weight of *Guernica* with the bright but uneasy smiles of the Sunflower Grin figure.

Collectors consider this drop one of the most meaningful VeVe charity events, combining English’s longstanding anti-war commentary with tangible humanitarian impact. Both works remain significant in the Popaganda digital canon as examples of socially conscious NFT art.
