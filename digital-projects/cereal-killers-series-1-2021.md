# 2021 – Ron English — *Cereal Killers Series 1* (Digital Trading Cards)

**Year:** 2021 (release date December 2021)  
**Platform:** Digital trading card NFT platforms (early Popaganda NFT wave)  
**Type:** Digital collectible card set (animated + static variants)

![Ron English — Cereal Killers Series 1](../images/Digital/cerealkillers.webp)

## Overview

**“Cereal Killers Series 1”** marks one of Ron English’s earliest large-scale digital releases, adapting the long-running *Cereal Killers* vinyl-toy universe into a card-based NFT format. The series includes animated and static versions featuring English’s satirical mascots—*Count Calorie*, *Fat Tony*, *Sugar Diabetic Tiger*, *Yankee Doodle Dandy*, *Two Ton Sam* and others—each parodying classic cereal-box characters through Popaganda’s dark comedic lens.

The set became a foundational moment in the early Popaganda NFT ecosystem. Many collectors who followed English from physical designer toys into Web3 cite the Series 1 drop as their entry point, and several characters from this lineup later reappeared in VeVe projects and cross-platform collaborations.
