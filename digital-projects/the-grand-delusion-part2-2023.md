# 2023 – *The Grand Delusion — Part 2* (Nifty Gateway)

**Year:** 2023 (March 23, 2023)  
**Platform:** Nifty Gateway – Curated  
**Type:** Multi-piece NFT collection continuation  

![The Grand Delusion — Part 2](../images/Digital/granddelusion2.webp)

## Overview

**The Grand Delusion — Part 2** continues the psychological and symbolic storytelling of the Delusionville universe, expanding the themes introduced in Part 1. This chapter introduces new character variations and surreal compositions built around **Popaganda social archetypes**, pushing Ron English’s motifs into darker and more introspective territory.

Many pieces from Part 2 play with contrasts between innocence and distortion, reflecting English’s long-running critique of media narratives, consumer psychology, and the contradictions embedded in cultural myths.

Collectors received additional incentives, including airdrop components and cross-series benefits for owning both parts. The two-part structure is now viewed as one of Ron English’s strongest digital-era narrative arcs.
